The Digital Product School offers the unique opportunity to develop state-of-the-art digital products while working in an interdisciplinary team. It is a 3 months full-time program. Digital Product School is a program provided by UnternehmerTUM GmbH in cooperation with Zentrum Digitalisierung Bayern.

UnternehmerTUM, a non-profit company, was founded in 2002. With more than 50 high-growth technology startups each year it is the leading center for business creation in Germany.
