PREAMBLE:
This agreement applies to the participation in an innovation format which is associated with the Digital Product School entity by UnternehmerTUM. This includes the:

  • Digital Product School offer itself, where talents work on challenges in collaboration with industry partners or TUM Venture Labs Star-tups or
  • Digitalschmiede Bayern offer, where talents work on challenges that are provided by the Bavarian State Ministry for Digital Affairs.
Both offers are hereafter referred to as “DPS programme”.

§ 1 Duration
The Participant will receive a scholarship for the time from {{batchTime}} as support during their participation in the DPS programme. The purpose of the grant is a monthly financial support in the amount of {{monthlyAmount}}.

§ 2 Obligations
The Student will participate in the DPS programme at the UnternehmerTUM GmbH and undertakes to complete it successfully. The Student is not employed by the company at the time of the program. The Participant is not participating in the DPS programme as a freelancer to use UnternehmerTUM as regular occupation but rather takes part at for education reasons. The Student has to ensure a proper internet connection with at least a real bandwidth of 10 Mbit/s in download and 2 Mbit/s upload.

§ 3 Total sum granted/Procedure for payments
(1) The student will be granted a scholarship amounting to a total of {{totalAmount}}.

(2) Payment will be effected in three monthly rates of {{monthlyAmount}} at the end of each month of participation (latest at the second workday of the following month) to the following bank account:

IBAN: {{iban}}
BIC: {{bic}}
Name of bank: {{bankName}}
Name of account holder: {{accountHolder}}

(3) For bank transfers to banks or bank accounts outside the European Union, UnternehmerTUM has to charge the participant a transfer fee of 15.00 euros per transfer (which means for 3 transfers 45.00 euros in total). The transfer fee will be directly subtracted from the monthly scholarship before the payment is transferred. This fee reduces the effective payment to {{bankAmount}} per transaction.
Be aware that your bank might apply transfer fees, too, that will further decrease your available amount.

§ 4 Taxes/Social security contributions
The Participant is solely responsible for adherence to regulations regarding the tax law and the social security system in their current place of residence. It is the Participant's responsibility to ensure they have adequate health insurance.

§ 5 Obligation regarding notification of sponsorship through UnternehmerTUM
In the event of publication of papers, conference agendas, contributions to conferences, essays etc, the Participant is obliged to quote, orally and in writing, Digital Product School as their sponsor.

§ 6 Liability
The Participant is liable for any damage due to negligence: They are responsible for the necessary insurance cover.

§ 7 Termination
(1) This contract can be terminated for good cause. This includes among other things:
- if one of the decisive requirements for the granting of the scholarship no longer applies,
- major breach of contract,
- if the Participant obviously fails to commit themselves to achieving the agreed target, e.g. absence without valid excuse, lack of active participation during tuition or in project work.
- if the Participant can’t ensure a sufficient internet connection for a trouble-free communication, e.g. essential video calls without any stocking, interruptions, and latency.

(2) In the event that the agreement is terminated on the grounds of the Participant’s conduct, then payments that have already been granted are to be reimbursed.

§ 8 Amendments and supplements to the agreement
All amendments and supplements to this agreement must be made in written form.
