To Digitalschmiede Bayern Batch#{{batch}}!

Digitalschmiede Bayern offers the unique opportunity to develop state of the art digital products in interdisciplinary teams within a three month timeframe. Participants range from employees of Bavarian Ministries to highly motivated and skilled university students and recent graduates. Together you will tackle real-world problems from the Bavarian Ministries and shape the digitalization of the Bavarian government.

Digitalschmiede Bayern is a program of the Bavarian State Ministry for Digital Affairs in cooperation with Digital Product School. Digital Product School is a program provided by UnternehmerTUM GmbH.

UnternehmerTUM, a non-profit company, was founded in 2002. With more than 50 high-growth technology startups each year it is the leading center for business creation in Germany.

The program takes place in the Munich Urban Colab (Freddie-Mercury-Straße 5, 80797 München) and runs from {{batchTime}}. The expected workload is 40 hours per week. {{scholarship}}
